Thesis

My PhD thesis is on age divides in political behaviour and utilises age-period-cohort modeling. My interst in age as a key determinanet of voter choice was initially sparked during the 2016 EU referendum, in which I was one of many young voters on the losing side. Since then, events in the UK and abroad have confirmed the growing importance of age in voter behaviour. My research seeks to discover the driving forces underpinning this change.
